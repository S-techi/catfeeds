Cat Studies & Research

Explore research-backed insights that shape how we feed, understand, and care for cats worldwide.

Study Overview

Protein Importance

Research shows that animal-based proteins help cats build lean muscles and stay energetic.

Hydration Habits

Cats on wet diets stay hydrated more easily, reducing urinary tract issues significantly.

Balanced Nutrition

Taurine, Omega-3, and Vitamin E are proven to support heart, coat, and eye health.

Key Findings

Wet Food Longevity

Studies link wet diets to longer lifespan and improved hydration.

Taurine Benefits

Feline studies confirm taurine is essential for healthy vision and strong cardiac function.

Natural Diets

Cats thrive on diets that closely mimic their natural prey composition.
